Course Outline:

  • Weeks 1-2: Intro to Combinatorics
    • Classic combinatorial problems
    • Set theory, bijection, & double counting
    • Essential principles (pigeonhole, multiplication, etc.)
    • Permutations & combinations
  • Weeks 3-5: Elements of Counting
    • Counting practicum
    • Applying principles
    • Combinatorial models
  • Weeks 6-9: Advanced Methods
    • Generating functions
    • Inclusion-Exclusion
    • Recurrence relations
  • Week 10: Spring Break
  • Weeks 11-13: Probability, Graphs, and Ramsey Theory
    • Probability basics & the probabilistic method
    • Graph theory basics & Ramsey Theory
  • Weeks 14-15: Special Topics & Student Presentations
    • Spectral graph theory
    • Social choice theory, Gerrymandering & redistricting
    • Student presentations
